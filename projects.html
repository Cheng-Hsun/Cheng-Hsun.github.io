I started to work as a research assistant under Dr. Shang-Hong Lai's instruction in my senior year. You can learn more about him and his Lab here. The following are the three projects I had worked on.

A Smart TV System with Multiple Human Computer Interaction Techniques

We applied gaze estimation, gesture recognition and facial expression recognition algorithm to a smart TV system. I was mainly in charge of the development of gaze estimation algorithm and the integration of system. We used a deep learning approach and the information of head pose to improve the accuracy of our algorithm. We achieved 8.6 mean error degree on MPIIGaze dataset. Our system was based on a 4k camera and a Kinect Sensor as shown in the above video.


Iris Recognition

This is a cooperation project with Novatek. We applied FCN model and ResNet-18 model to accomplish iris segmentation and recognition. And I managed to use an iris scanner (IriShield MK 2120U) and its SDK to develop an iris recognition system. I used image processing techniques to close the gap between training data of the models and images captured by the device. I also integrated different models by cloud computing.

4K 360 degree Panorama Video

This is a cooperation project with Industrial Technology Research Institute. We applied OpenCV to develop image stitching algorithms for real-time panorama videos. I designed a color correction approach to eliminate visual artifacts and applied CUDA programming and OpenMP to accelerate the algorithm. Our algorithm is able to stitch a 4k panorama image in 0.03 second with GeForce GTX TITAN X.

When I was in my junior year, I managed a team of three members and conducted our capstone project under Dr. Szu-Hao Huang's instruction. Dr. Huang is an enthusiastic professor and is always willing to help students. You can find more information about him and his Lab here.



Improve the visual quality of smartphone camera using light sensors and computer vision algorithms

We developed an Android application that can retouch selfies. We use light sensors to capture the color of environmental light and accomplish white balance by this information. We also collected hundreds of selfies from different countries and used computer vision algorithms to obtain the representative colors of faces. Then our application can help users get their desired selfies.
